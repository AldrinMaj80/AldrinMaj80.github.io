Why Vote Tertuis?

A DA Value based Leader with foresight

    My Vision

  • To be a regional leader exemplifying the ideal practice of providing transparency, accountability and servant leadership to our region.

    My Mission

  • In order to realize my vision I will:
  • Communicate the decisions of the regional structure
  • See that all constituencies of the DA carry out their duties properly.

    My Strategic Objectives

  • In order to realize my vision I will pursue the following key objectives:
  • To establish and sustain a relationship of trust and support between our public representatives, structures,activists and communities
  • To promote the brand of the DA and establish the party as the most viable political choice
  • To maintain excellence, integrity, commitment, teamwork, trust, accountability and professionalism
  • To identify pockets of success in other DA structures and to draw on their expertise
  • Maximizing the DA’s share of vote
